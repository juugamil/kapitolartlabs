    Tagging & Labeling

    We know apparel here at the Lab. Apparel design is one of our specialties; & with that, comes the ability to create various trims for your clothing label. Hang-Tags, Neck Tags, Hem-Tags, Jock-Tags, etc., create larger marketability for both your products and your brand. We can create a tagging package that is on-brand & ready for production. Your trim file will allow you to send your tag designs to major production with technical directions that can be interpreted by both domestic and foreign production. From our own experience running brands & collecting feedback from clients, we’ve seen what investing into quality label design to your products can do for a brand. In most cases, you will see an increase of interaction/purchases from your ideal customer as well as an increase in general interaction with your company.
